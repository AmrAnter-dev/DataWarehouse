High-level Overview

The objective of this project is to design and implement a Retail Sales Data Warehouse (DW) and Business Intelligence (BI) system that transforms raw transactional data into structured, query-optimized, and decision-ready datasets.

The system focuses on ingesting raw operational transactional data, processing it through a layered architecture, and delivering analytical insights related to revenue, product performance, and operational efficiency.

At its core, the project aims to evolve from raw data ingestion (Bronze layer) to a fully modelled dimensional data warehouse (Gold layer) that supports scalable reporting and analytics.
